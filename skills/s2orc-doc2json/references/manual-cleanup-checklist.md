# Manual Cleanup Checklist

Use this after conversion when the Markdown needs review.

- Confirm the title is the paper title, not a journal banner or page header.
- Remove repeated headers, footers, and page-number noise when the repetition is obvious.
- Check that section headings are in plausible reading order.
- Remove duplicated paragraphs introduced by the parser.
- Repair obvious broken line wraps inside paragraphs.
- Keep citations exactly as recovered unless they are clearly duplicated noise.
- Keep figure and table captions when available; do not fabricate missing captions.
- Do not rewrite equations or symbols from memory.
- If a section is severely degraded, leave the best recovered text and report the limitation explicitly.
